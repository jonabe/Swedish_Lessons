**Part C: Activity / Reinforcement**

* **Activity 1: Label the Family**  
  * Draw a simple picture of your family. Write the correct Swedish word (mamma, pappa, syster, bror, jag) next to each person.  
* **Activity 2: Fill in the Blanks**  
  * Use min, din, or mina (my, plural) to complete the sentences.  
  1. Det här är \_\_\_\_\_ bror. (This is **my** brother.)  
  2. Jag älskar \_\_\_\_\_ föräldrar. (I love **my** parents.)  
  3. Är det där \_\_\_\_\_ mamma? (Is that **your** mom?)

**Part D: "Extra Reps"**

* **Language Tip:** The word for "my" changes if the noun is plural. For one brother, you say "**min** bror". For multiple parents, you say "**mina** föräldrar".

**Part E: Advanced / Challenge Section**

* **Challenge:** Introduce the word for "but" (men). Create a Swedish sentence that says "I have a brother, but I don't have a sister." (Hint: Use Jag har en bror, men jag har ingen syster.)

**Part D: "Extra Reps"**
